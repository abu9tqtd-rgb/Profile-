01 — About

Precision engineering,
directed by design instinct.

JC
Abubaker — Telangana, Hyderabad

I spend my days at the intersection of engineering rigor and design taste — building products for founders and creative teams who need their software to look and feel as considered as their brand.

Over the last eleven years I've led front-end architecture for venture-backed startups, art-directed motion for consumer apps, and shipped e-commerce platforms processing eight figures in annual revenue. I care less about frameworks and more about the 200 tiny decisions that make an interface feel inevitable.

When I'm not building, I'm usually reverse-engineering the interaction design of my favorite products, or mentoring engineers making the jump from "it works" to "it's remarkable."
